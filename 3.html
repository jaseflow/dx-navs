Top Los Angeles neighborhoods

Beach Cities

Find endless summer on the boardwalks or catch some waves on the beaches of these laid-back waterfront communities.

Santa Monica

An up-market city home to surfers, students, and celebrities, this is where to enjoy one of the L.A. area's famous beaches, a historic pier and great nightlife.

Beverly Hills - West Hollywood

Designer luxury and expensive opulence are the hallmarks of this upscale neighborhood, anchored by the top-notch shopping on Rodeo Drive.

Downtown Los Angeles

Once a hardworking industrial neighborhood, today’s Downtown LA is a destination reborn with an emphasis on culture and green spaces.

Hollywood

From glittering Walk of Fame to the brilliant theater marquees, everything about Hollywood screams star power. You'll find the best celebrity hangouts here.

Long Beach

Best known as a heart of industry, Long Beach also boasts a newly revived downtown core, plenty of bars and restaurants and beautiful beaches.
